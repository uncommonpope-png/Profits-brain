# PLT Press Conversion Funnel Optimization Analysis

## Current Funnel Analysis

### 🎯 **Current Conversion Path**
1. **Entry Point**: Index page with individual book listings
2. **Bundle Promotion**: Small banner at top ($49 bundle, 70% savings)
3. **Decision Conflict**: Individual books vs. Bundle choice
4. **Payment**: Stripe checkout for $49 bundle

### 📊 **Current Issues Detected**

#### 1. **Choice Paralysis Problem**
- Landing page shows 18+ individual books ($7.99-$14.99 each)
- Bundle option competes with individual purchases
- Users might buy 1-2 books instead of $49 bundle
- **Revenue Loss**: $7.99-$15.98 vs. $49 bundle**

#### 2. **Weak Value Proposition Display**
- Bundle savings (70%) buried in small banner
- Individual book prices make bundle seem expensive
- No clear "best value" messaging hierarchy

#### 3. **Multiple Call-to-Action Dilution**
- 18 individual "Buy Now" buttons
- 1 bundle CTA competes with 18 others
- User attention scattered
