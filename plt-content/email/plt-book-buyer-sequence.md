Before any major business decision, ask:

1. **PROFIT**: Does this create sustainable value?

   - Is it scalable without breaking me?
   - Does it solve a real problem people pay for?

2. **LOVE**: Will this energize or drain me?
   - Am I excited to wake up and work on this?
   - Does this play to my strengths?
   - Will I still enjoy this in 2 years?

3. **TAX**: What's the optimal structure?
   - How should this be legally organized?
   - What are the tax implications?
   - How do I minimize risk and maximize efficiency?

**If ANY answer is "no" or "I don't know" → you're not ready to proceed.**

**PLT STACK RANKING (Quick Method)**

1. Score opportunity: Profit __/10, Love __/10, Tax __/10
2. Weight based on your priorities (1x, 2x, or 3x)
3. Calculate: (P×weight) + (L×weight) + (T×weight)
4. Choose highest total that passes decision filter
