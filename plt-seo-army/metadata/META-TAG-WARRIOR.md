### TITLE TAG OPTIMIZATION RULES

**1. Primary Keywords First**
- Lead with "Profit Love Tax" or "PLT" 
- Include "Framework" for broader reach
- Add year for freshness signals

**2. Character Count Optimization**
- Target: 50-60 characters
- Maximum: 60 characters
- Include brand when space allows

**3. Compelling Modifiers**
- "Complete Guide"
- "Step-by-Step" 
- "Proven Strategies"
- "2026 Update"
- "Master Class"

**4. Title Variations by Intent**
- **Informational:** "What is PLT: Complete Guide"
