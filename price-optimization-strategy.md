## 🧪 **PRICE TESTING FRAMEWORK**

### **Testing Methodology**
- **Platform**: A/B testing with traffic splitting
- **Duration**: 2-week minimum per test
- **Sample Size**: Minimum 1,000 visitors per variant
- **Success Metric**: **Total Revenue** (not conversion rate)

### **Key Formula**
**Revenue = Traffic × Conversion Rate × Price**
- Higher price might reduce conversion but increase total revenue
- Lower price might increase volume but reduce total revenue
