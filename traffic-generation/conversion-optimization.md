## A/B TESTING ROADMAP

### Month 1 Tests
1. **Headline Test:** "3-Number System" vs. "PLT Framework"
2. **Price Anchor:** $49 vs. $49 (was $200)
3. **CTA Color:** Blue vs. Orange vs. Green
4. **Social Proof:** Testimonials vs. Numbers vs. Case Studies

### Month 2 Tests
1. **Landing Page Format:** Long form vs. Short form
2. **Email Opt-in:** Exit intent vs. Time-based vs. Scroll-based
3. **Guarantee:** 30-day vs. 60-day vs. No-questions-asked
4. **Bundle Presentation:** Grid vs. List vs. Carousel

### Month 3 Tests
1. **Pricing Strategy:** $49 vs. $39 vs. $59
2. **Payment Options:** One-time vs. Payment plan
3. **Bonus Offers:** Free consultation vs. Extra books
4. **Email Subject Lines:** 5 variations per campaign
