## CONTENT CREATION PROCESS

### Research Phase (Week 1)
- Analyze target publication's recent articles
- Identify content gaps and trending topics
- Research publication's style and formatting
- Find editor contact information
- Create publication-specific angle

### Pitch Phase (Week 2)  
- Send personalized pitch to 5 publications
- Follow up after 1 week if no response
- Track open rates and response rates
- Adjust pitch based on feedback

### Writing Phase (Week 3)
- Create detailed outline
- Write compelling introduction with hook
- Include 2-3 PLT case studies
- Add actionable takeaways
- Include author bio with PLT Press link

### Optimization Phase (Week 4)
- Edit for publication's style
- Add internal links to PLT content
- Optimize for SEO (target keywords)
- Include calls-to-action for email signup
- Submit final draft
